Data Storage
Nothing is saved on the extension, so removing the extension won't remove your data. But reinstalling the browser will remove the data.
All your Roblokis data is stored at your Roblox website on the Local Storage. So your data is as safe as your Roblox token. Which shouldn't be showen to anyone!

Roblokis doesn't store any Roblox data!

Here is what data roblokis store;
- Enabled and Disabled Features.
- Features Data. (Text for Custom Robux & Name)
- Custom Themes. (Themes created by you)
- Current Theme. (Is witch theme to load)

That is all the stored data by Roblokis.
This site will be updated if roblokis store more stuff!
Data Usage
Currently all data for the extension remains in Roblox website. All personal and Roblox data is kept inside the site and is not sent outside of Roblox.

Any extension that has access to roblox.com has access to everything you can do,
including but not limited to:
- Your Robux
- Your invntory
- Your games
- Your friends list
- Your groups
- Your Tokens
- Everything.

does Robloxkis need access to all of this?
Mostly it doesn't.
None of this data is stored.
But some of it is used by the extension.

As an example: The extension uses your Roblox id to check badges achieve date to show it on badges.

Another example: The extension gets your token to remove friends for Quick Friend Remover feature.

Last example: The extension join Roblox games for you by clicking the Quick Join button on any game.

If you don't trust me as a developer who use this data securely then please don't use this extension.
Permissions
Roblokis needs access to the roblox.com website to modify the website.
Without access to the website features and themes won't work.

Other permissions like accessing other sites is not required
but is used (when requested by the user) to retrieved image content from those sites!
